BBC Sport - Winter Olympics: GB's Jamie Nicholls crashes out in second slopestyle qualification run
GB's Nicholls crashes out in slopestyle qualifiers
10 Feb 2018
From the section Winter Olympics
GB's Jamie Nicholls is out of the men's slopestyle event after crashing out in his second run in the qualifiers. Nicholls finished eighth after the two runs with only the top six making the final.
