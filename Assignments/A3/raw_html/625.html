    The Winter Olympics kicked off in Pyeongchang, South Korea, with an epic Opening Ceremony. The broadcast aired Friday night on NBC and began trending on Twitter.

    There were numerous Opening Ceremony highlights, including a shirtless Tongan, the Jamaican athletes' dancing entrance and the blank look on the face of Kim Yo-jong, the sister of North Korean leader Kim Jong-Un, when Vice President Mike Pence applauded as the United States delegation was introduced.
