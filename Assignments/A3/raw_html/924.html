Kim Kardashian Stuns in KKW Beauty on the Cover of 'Vogue' Taiwan

The reality star talks her beauty empire.

By Tora Northman/Feb 8, 2018/Beauty
1,438 Hypes

Social media mogul, reality star and entrepreneur Kim Kardashian covers the February issue of Vogue Taiwan. Wearing a full face of her own KKW Beauty line, Kardashian talks building her empire, her love for cosmetics and the beginning of her brand. She also touches on her recent fragrance addition, revealing that she has always been fascinated by scent saying, “Fragrance represents your mood, and can also reveal a person’s personality.”

To read the full interview, head over to Vogue Taiwan, and click through the gallery above to see all the images the feature. Don’t miss Kim on the cover of ODDA Magazine while you’re at it.
