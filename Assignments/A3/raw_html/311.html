Kylie Jenner's Ex-Boyfriend Tyga Reportedly Wants A DNA Test For Stormi Webster

Tyga thinks he might be the dad.

By Victoria Rodriguez
Feb 9, 2018
Getty Images

Kylie Jenner's baby Stormi Webster arrived just a couple of days ago, and there's already so much drama! Kylie's ex-boyfriend Tyga reportedly believes he, NOT Travis Scott, could be Stormi's real dad, and he's even demanding a DNA test, according to Radar Online.

“Tyga told friends that he was still sexually active with Kylie nine months ago, when this baby was likely conceived” a source close to the rapper said.

And this isn't the first time Tyga has spoken out about the baby. Back in September, when the pregnancy rumors started going around, he took to Snapchat and wrote, “Hell nah that’s my kid.”

Kylie has yet to respond to these comments, but stay tuned for updates.
